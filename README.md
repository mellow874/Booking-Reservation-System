This website is based on a Booking and Reservation system for the Billy-G Restaurant 
It consists of both frontend and backend.
It is built using Reactjs, Tailwindcss, Nodejs, Express and Supabase.
Upon opening the website we've got the homepage, which is where you will get to know about the restaurant and what they offer, and it consists of different sections and links that lead to different pages, and also the social media icons.
As you scroll down you will come across the Book Now button, which when you click will lead you to the booking and reservation system.
The first page of the Booking and Reservation system is the Booking page, which has the calendar, where you get to pick a date and note that past dates and weekends are not available, the you have the dropdowns for the number of guests, time slots and seating preferences.
Once you've chosen your preferences and click the next button which will lead to the Details page.
The details page is whereby the customer has to fill in their personal details (name, surname, email, address, and special requests).
The next page is the confirm detail page where you get to see all yhe information you put in and verify it then you may make your booking and it will alert the backend side that a booking has been made.
